### Technical Implementation Details

**Robots.txt** (`/public/robots.txt`):
- Allows `/` for all user agents
- Disallows `/_next/`, `/api/`, `/dashboard/`
- Host directive: `https://pixelversestudios.io`
- Sitemap declared

**Sitemap** (next-sitemap):
- 44+ URLs including all 10 priority cities
- Change frequency: daily for homepage (priority 1.0), weekly for all others (priority 0.7)
- Generated files: `/public/sitemap.xml` (index) and `/public/sitemap-0.xml` (content)

**Structured Data** (`/lib/structured-data.ts`):
- LocalBusiness schema (ProfessionalService type) with NAP, geo coordinates, social links
- Per-city LocalBusiness schemas for all 10 cities with unique coordinates
- Service schema (Web Design, Local SEO, UX/UI Design) per city
- BreadcrumbList on all service and blog pages
- FAQPage schema on /faq and city pages
- BlogPosting schema on all blog posts
