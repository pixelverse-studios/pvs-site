## 3. Target Audience

### Primary Audience Profile

**Demographics:**

- Small to medium business owners in Bergen County, NJ
- Revenue: $100K - $5M annually
- Employees: 1-50
- Decision-maker: Owner, partner, or marketing manager

**Industries:**

- **Legal:** Solo practitioners, small law firms (family, personal injury, real estate)
- **Medical:** Practices, clinics, dentists, specialists, senior care
- **Professional Services:** CPAs, real estate agents, consultants, financial advisors
- **Local Services:** Contractors, HVAC, plumbers, electricians, home services

**Pain Points:**

- Website is outdated or doesn't reflect brand quality
- Not appearing in local Google searches
- Losing leads to competitors with better online presence
- Previous agency used templates that limit customization
- Need a website that converts visitors to customers

**Search Behavior:**

- Searches "[service] + [city] NJ" (e.g., "web design Fort Lee NJ")
- Asks "How much does a website cost?"
- Compares template builders vs. custom development
- Values personal service over large agency bureaucracy
